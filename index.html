About

Real-time Urban Mapping and Food Delivery with Sidewalk Roaming Robots

  • April 2020

    Beginnings

    Following our experiences on BlueSky Solar Racing our team wanted to get together to work on a robotic learning project. Once we started piloting our robot over LTE we realized we could use it to pick-up and deliver goods.

  • June 2020

    The Prototype

    Equipped with cameras, LIDAR and an array of other sensors we realized that driving the robot around provided an opportunity to create high fidelity maps which in turn could bootstrap a semi automous control system for the robot.

  • July 2020

    Partnering and Testing

    We've made over a dozen deliveries to our friends from local diners and have partnered with CheaprEats as we work to iterate and improve out prototype.
